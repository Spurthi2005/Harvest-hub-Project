Support - HarvestHub

At HarvestHub, we're here to assist you every step of the way. Whether you're a farmer or a buyer, we offer comprehensive support to ensure your experience is seamless and successful. 1. For Farmers: - Easy Crop Listing: Upload videos and record crop weights with ease. - Direct Sales: No need to visit markets; sell from the comfort of your home. - Secure Payments: Choose from multiple payment methods, including UPI, online transfers, and COD. - Delivery Assistance: We help arrange delivery to the buyer's specified address. 2. For Buyers: - Wide Variety: Access fresh crops from trusted farmers across different regions. - Verified Listings: View videos and details of crops before making a purchase. - Flexible Payment Options: Pay online or via COD at your convenience. - Reliable Delivery: We ensure timely delivery of your purchased products.
