How It Works

For Farmers

1. Register and log in.

2. Upload your crop’s details, including videos and weight.

3. Set your price and list your produce.

4. We help arrange delivery after a buyer purchases.

For Buyers

1. Browse available crops and select the ones you want.

2. Add to cart and choose your payment option.

3. Get your crops delivered to your doorstep.
